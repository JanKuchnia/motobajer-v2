## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**LLM assessment:** Partially AI-looking. Palette and typography are on-brand for the automotive brief. Breaks down with: 6 uppercase tracked eyebrows in ContactSection, the 2×2 stat grid (big-number template), and placeholder gallery images. These are the reflex tells.

**Deterministic scan:** 2 findings.
1. `single-font` (BaseLayout.astro:56) — Barlow + Barlow Condensed are same family, no genuine typographic contrast. Real finding.
2. `layout-transition` (global.css:216) — `transition: width` on nav underline pseudo-element. Semi-false positive (negligible performance impact on decorative element).

## Overall Impression

Solid bones — committed palette, real accessibility work, excellent mobile sticky CTA. Hurt by template reflexes in mid-section: eyebrow overuse, stat grid template, placeholder gallery. Biggest single opportunity: real workshop photos.

## What's Working

1. Mobile sticky CTA bar (WhatsApp + phone) with safe-area insets — exactly right for this brief.
2. Section background alternation — creates rhythm across the long scroll.
3. Hero content hierarchy — tight information order matches mobile snap-decision scanning.

## Priority Issues

**[P0] Gallery ships placeholder images**
Real workshop photos are trust, not decoration. Placeholder rectangles read as a broken site.
Fix: Real photos of shop floor, lift, storefront, parts counter.
Suggested command: /impeccable harden

**[P1] Mailto form silently fails on mobile**
Most Android phones have no configured email app. User fills form, hits submit, nothing happens. Conversion lost.
Fix: Upgrade to Formspree (code comment at ContactSection.astro:57 has exact instructions).
Suggested command: /impeccable harden

**[P1] Two h2 headings in ContactSection**
"Zarezerwuj wizytę" and "Kontakt" both h2 with identical scale. Screen readers see two equal-weight sections. Right column heading should be h3.
Fix: Demote "Kontakt" heading to h3, reduce visual scale.
Suggested command: /impeccable clarify

**[P2] Uppercase tracked eyebrow overuse**
6 instances in ContactSection + 2 in Footer. Absolute ban violation — scaffolding, not voice.
Fix: Replace with heavier weight normal-case labels or simple dividers.
Suggested command: /impeccable typeset

**[P2] About section stat grid is the big-number template**
18 lat / 2 punkty / 5.0★ / EuroWarsztat — four cells of big-number + label. The EuroWarsztat cell forces "Euro" to serve as the big number. Absolute ban.
Fix: Replace with horizontal trust-statement row, pull-quote of the strongest stat, or integrated callouts within copy.
Suggested command: /impeccable layout

## Minor Observations

- Inline onmouseover/onmouseout handlers on buttons conflict with transition-colors; replace with CSS :hover.
- Scroll indicator at opacity-40 is nearly invisible; adds clutter for minimal navigation value.
- FAQ section on homepage has no nav anchor.
- backdrop-filter: blur(8px) on nav-scrolled is expensive on low-end Android.
- Gallery CSS columns + IO reveal stagger can fail for off-screen columns; 2000ms safety net catches it but FOUC window exists.
- section-heading scale is identical across all sections; primary CTA should outsize peripheral sections.
